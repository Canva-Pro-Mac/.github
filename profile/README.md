## Overview

Canva Pro Advanced is an all-in-one graphic design and content creation platform that enables anyone to create professional-quality social media graphics, presentations, videos, print materials, and marketing content through an intuitive visual editor with thousands of templates, millions of stock assets, and AI-powered design tools. It democratizes graphic design by providing the templates, assets, and tools that produce professional results without requiring design training or expensive specialized software.

Template library provides thousands of professionally designed templates for every common design format — social media posts and stories for every platform, presentations, business cards, flyers, posters, invitations, resumes, and dozens of other formats. Templates are fully customizable with drag-and-drop editing that replaces placeholder content with your own text, images, and branding. New templates are added continuously to cover emerging formats and trends.

Brand Kit maintains organizational brand assets — logos, colors, fonts, and approved imagery — in a centralized location that is accessible in every design, ensuring visual consistency across all created content. The brand kit automatically suggests brand colors and fonts when editing, making brand-compliant design the path of least resistance. Multiple brand kits accommodate agencies and consultants who manage multiple brands.

AI-powered tools generate images from text descriptions, remove backgrounds from photos, resize designs for different platforms automatically, generate text content, and suggest design improvements. Magic Eraser removes unwanted elements from photos, and Magic Resize adapts a design for different social media platforms with intelligent layout adjustment. The AI tools handle the tedious aspects of design production.

For marketers, small business owners, social media managers, and anyone who needs to create professional visual content without design expertise, Canva Pro Advanced is the design platform that makes professional-quality visual content creation accessible to everyone.

## Performance

Canva Pro balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
